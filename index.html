You will be presented with two combatants in the form of movies from the American Film Institute's Top 100 American Movies.

To beatdown your opponent and reward your hero (i.e. the movie you prefer), simply click your favorite of the two.

A progress meter is displayed under your combatants. It may take awhile, but persevere and you'll end up a ranking of all your heroes and their fallen foes.

To commence your Movie Beatdown, simply click the play button below.
